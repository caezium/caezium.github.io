>[!def] Power
>[[work]] over time

- Power ($P$), is the **measure of [[work]] done per unit time**.
- Measured in **watts** ($W$).
- **Scalar** quantity.

#### $Power = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{\Delta E}{t}$
