[[Factors of Production]]
also referred to as capital [[Goods]] and producer [[Goods]], the manufactured resources requires in the production process, such as machinery, tools, equipment, and vehicles. production buildings are capital, not land
**Reward** - interest


[[Mobility of Factors of Production]]
**Capital mobility**
Capital equipment can be transferred to one or another and might be used for different purposes too.
ex. geographically immobile Mine, mobile photocopier. occupationally mobile delivery van, immobile oil rig.



[[Mobility of Factors of Production]]
#### Quantity
investment and tend to increase with time
	old capital [[Goods]] will be worn out or become outdated, needing new capital [[Goods]] to replace the old ones.
		the total value of the output of the capital [[Goods]] is **gross investment**. the value of the replaced capital good is called **depreciation**. net investment is the value of extra capital [[Goods]] that have been made.
