Upward pressure on price: occurs when demand exceeds supply.
downward pressure on price: supply exceeds demand.



## effects of changes in [[Demand]]
higher price -> extension  in [[Supply]] until it reaches a new [[what/02_areas/ECON/9/Equilibrium]] price

![[IMG_5259.jpeg]]

decrease in [[Demand]] -> contraction[[Supply| in su]]pply, fall in price
![[IMG_5260.jpeg]]



## effects of changes in [[Supply]]
changes in [[Supply]] causes a change in price and a movement along the [[Demand]] curve
initially, an increase in [[Supply]] will cause a surplus -> drives down the price, resulting in an extension in [[Demand]].

decrease in [[Supply]] -> rise in price, contraction in [[Demand]]
![[IMG_5261.jpeg]]


# changes in [[Demand]] and [[Supply]]
it is possible for the conditions of [[Demand]] and the conditions of [[Supply]] to change at the same time. 

so
![[IMG_5262.jpeg]]



more sale taxes shifts [[Supply]] curve inward -> [[what/02_areas/ECON/9/Equilibrium]] price raised



> [!summary] 
> - an increase in [[Demand]] -> raises price and extension in [[Supply]]
> - a decrease in [[Demand]] -> lowers prices and contraction in [[Supply]]
> - an increase in [[Supply]] -> lowers the price and extension in [[Demand]]
> - a decrease in [[Supply]] -> raises the price and contraction in [[Demand]]
>
