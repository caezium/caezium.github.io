>[!def]  Element
>A substance made of atoms that all contain the **same number of protons** and cannot be split into anything simpler


>[!def] Compound
>two or more different elements held together by **chemical bonds**



>[!def] Mixture
>mixture of different types of **molecule or atom**, which is not ***chemically combined***
