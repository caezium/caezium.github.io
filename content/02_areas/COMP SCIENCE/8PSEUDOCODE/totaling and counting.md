## Task 2

Program that will keep letting me add money to a piggy bank until I hit 100

```pseudocode

DECLARE bank : REAL

Bank <- 0.00

WHILE bank < 100 DO

                  OUTPUT “add money”

                  DECLARE in : REAL

                  INPUT in

                  Bank <- bank + in

ENDWHILE

OUPUT bank

```
