    Speed

    distance travelled per Δ time. Scalar. does not depend on the direction speed=timedistance​

    Velocity

    the Speed in a given direction. Vector. change in displacement, has direction. vˉ=ts​

    Acceleration

    the rate at which an object increases speed or velocity, change in velocity, vector. a=tv−u​
